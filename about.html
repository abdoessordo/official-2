We are CSAM

SIHA is a Moroccan quick-commerce start-up founded in Casablanca in 2020. It is an on-demand courier service that purchases, picks up, and delivers products ordered through its mobile app. It aspires to be a multi-category lifestyle app with food delivery being the most popular offering. In fact, The company has a vision to be a lifestyle app with all urban services available easily through its smartphone application. Food delivery service remains its most popular service. Other services available on the app include Groceries, Pharmacy, ... The food business allows users to find and place orders with their favourite restaurants which is picked up when ready and delivered to the user's doorstep.

The SIHA logo symbolizes health which was our main objective of foundation, and it reflects also an abbreviation of its founders names.

Our objective

SIHA is the app that allows you to get the best products of your city in a few minutes. We connect users, businesses and couriers to make that possible. The project was born during the coronavirus with the aim of transforming the way users acquire what they need, making cities more accessible. At SIHA we want to give everyone easy access to anything in their city.

Why choose us ?

Secure Payment ~ SIHA is linked to several secure payment systems such as : Visa, Mastercard... These credit card payment systems are subject to a proven SSL type security system. We have adopted the encryption process which is the current reference in data encryption on the Internet. All sensitive data related to payment methods are thus protected. We accept two currencies of payment: Moroccan Dirham and the Euro and the US dollar. You can still use wafacach or tashilat to pay your purchases

The Delivery ~ At home : Delivery in your mailbox. In case of absence, you have the choice, by logging on our site, between a new delivery date within 6 working days or a deposit of your package from the next day at 3pm in our post office of your choice. By default. You can contact us in case of delay in the request, or report it on our SIHA site, so that we can compensate you. In your current location : With SIHA, we can deliver your order to your current location, starting at 15 minutes, depending on your location, with a reduction in the order price in case of delay.
