Our vision is to make sport enjoyable to every one !

We are CSAM

The Arts et Métiers Sports Club is a club created by engineering students from the National School of Arts and Crafts of Casablanca which ensures the practice of sport and organizes several optional physical and sports activities for all students of the normal cycle. This club makes every effort to satisfy all students; both those who want to practice their passion, and those who want to keep in shape only. Our mission is simple: to promote the practice of sport as an irreplaceable hobby, to discover new sports hitherto unrecognized, even unseen, to increase competition between students from a sporting point of view, and to raise the level of university sport and itself. give a new lease of life.
